 Almond Liqueur

Recipe By     : 
Serving Size  : 1     Preparation Time : 0:00
Categories    : Alcoholic Beverages              
  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
       3           cup  sugar
   2 1/4           cup  water
       3                lemons; the rind -- finely
                        grated
       1         quart  vodka
       3    tablespoon  almond extract
       2    tablespoon  vanilla extract

Combine first 3 ingredients in a Dutch oven; bring to a boil. 
Reduce heat and simmer 5 minutes, stirring occasionally; cool completely. 
Stir in remaining ingredients; store in airtight containers.  
Yield: about 6 1/2 cups.
